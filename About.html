COMPANY OBJECTIVE

Our companys objective is customer satisfaction, we are committed to providing quality and satisfactory work, according to specifications and on time so that it can support customer operational activities.

Through technological innovation, continuous improvement and our experience, dedicated and motivated people, we are committed to the best achievement.

WHO WE ARE

CV. CITRA DIMAS SEJAHTERA is a company engaged in the repair service contractor. CV Citra Dimas Sejahtera was established in 2015 and is located in Bekasi City. Our services include: Electric Motor & Pump Services, Mechanical Services, Fabrication Services, IT Supplies & Services, General Trading. In producing quality repairs, our company is supported by trained personnel and adequate equipment and is always ready to serve.

VISSION

To become a leading contractor company in the field of repair, procurement, fabrication and technical assistance services to its customers.

MISSION

3. Committed to improvement, product quality and on time delivery
